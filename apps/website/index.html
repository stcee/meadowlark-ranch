Our Story

At its heart, the ranch brings together expert horse trainers, innovative equine therapeutic modalities, and transformative coaching—all set within an environment that prioritizes wellness, learning, and authentic community.

Heart of a Cowboy

Guests are invited to engage with horses in hands-on, educational, and therapeutic activities that nurture personal growth and collective well-being. By championing stewardship, education, and holistic care, Meadowlark Ranch stands as a unique haven for those seeking renewal, inspiration, and a deeper bond with both horses and one another.

Our mission to preserve rare horse breeds

Meadowlark Ranch is dedicated to thoughtfully preserving rare and heritage horse breeds, championing their ongoing legacy through education, stewardship, and immersive guest experiences.

Our mission integrates the well-being and conservation of these breeds with world-class hospitality and holistic wellness, inviting guests to become active participants in a meaningful cause.
