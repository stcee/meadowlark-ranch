## We Would Love to Hear From You

Whether you are planning a wedding, exploring equine-assisted healing, inquiring about horse boarding, or simply curious about life at Meadowlark Ranch, our team is here to help. We take pride in responding thoughtfully and promptly to every inquiry, because every conversation is the beginning of a potential relationship — and relationships are what Meadowlark Ranch is built on.

## How to Reach Us

We welcome inquiries of all kinds. To ensure you reach the right member of our team, here is a guide to our primary areas of service:

- **Event inquiries** — weddings, corporate events, private celebrations, and carriage services
- **Equine-assisted healing** — session scheduling, program information, and Hope Held By A Horse referrals
- **Horse boarding & transport** — availability, rates, and service area information
- **CCP Percherons & sponsorships** — competition schedule, sponsorship opportunities, and partnership inquiries
- **General questions** — ranch tours, media inquiries, volunteer opportunities, and anything else
