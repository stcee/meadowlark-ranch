## Find Your Way to Meadowlark Ranch

Meadowlark Ranch is located in Longmont, Colorado, in the heart of Boulder County along the scenic St. Vrain corridor. With the Flatiron Mountains rising to the west and the open prairie stretching eastward, the drive to the ranch is as beautiful as the destination itself.

We operate two locations, each serving a distinct facet of our mission:

## Our Locations

### Meadowlark Ranch — Main Property

**5384 St. Vrain Road, Longmont, CO 80503**

Our original homestead and the heart of the Meadowlark Ranch experience. This is where our event venues, equine-assisted healing programs, and heritage horse facilities are located. Set along the picturesque St. Vrain Road corridor, the property offers panoramic views of the Flatiron Mountains and the Continental Divide.

### Meadowlark Ranch Too — Percheron Facility

**10500 N 49th Street, Longmont, CO 80503**

Our purpose-built facility dedicated to the CCP Percherons program. This state-of-the-art property features custom draft horse barns, training arenas, and the six-horse hitch practice area. Visits to Meadowlark Ranch Too are available by appointment.

## Driving Directions

### From Denver (approximately 33 miles / 42 minutes)

Take **I-25 North** to **Exit 243 (Highway 66 West / Longmont)**. Head west on Highway 66 through Longmont. Continue west past Main Street and follow Highway 66 as it curves through town. Turn north on **75th Street**, then west on **St. Vrain Road**. Meadowlark Ranch will be on your right.

### From Boulder (approximately 16 miles / 20 minutes)

Take **Highway 119 (Diagonal Highway)** northeast toward Longmont. As you enter Longmont, continue on Highway 119 which becomes **Main Street**. Turn west on **Highway 66**, then follow the directions above to **St. Vrain Road**. A beautiful and direct drive through the heart of Boulder County.

### From Fort Collins (approximately 40 miles / 45 minutes)

Take **I-25 South** to **Exit 243 (Highway 66 West / Longmont)**. Head west on Highway 66 and follow the same route as the Denver directions above. Alternatively, take **Highway 287 South** to Longmont for a more scenic route along the Front Range.

### From Denver International Airport (approximately 45 miles / 50 minutes)

From DIA, take **Pena Boulevard West** to **I-70 West**, then merge onto **I-25 North**. Follow the Denver directions above from I-25 North. For visitors flying in for events, the drive from the airport to the ranch offers a beautiful introduction to Colorado's Front Range landscape.

## A Note About the Drive

The approach to Meadowlark Ranch along St. Vrain Road is one of the most scenic corridors in Boulder County. As you leave the bustle of town behind, the road winds through open agricultural land with the Rocky Mountains filling the western horizon. In spring, the meadows are alive with wildflowers and birdsong. In autumn, the cottonwood trees along the St. Vrain Creek blaze with gold. In winter, the snow-capped peaks create a tableau of quiet grandeur.

Take a moment to slow down and enjoy the drive. The pace of Meadowlark Ranch begins well before you arrive at the gate.
